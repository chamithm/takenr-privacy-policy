# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 19, 2026

Welcome to **TakenR**. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("TakenR") and associated services.

By using the TakenR application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our rental management service, we may collect the following types of information:

* **Account & Business Information:** When you sign up and configure your business, we collect your shop name, username, contact number, and shop address.
* **Rental Inventory & Transaction Data:** Information about rental items, categories, pricing, stock levels, and order histories that you record in the application.
* **Customer Information:** Information about your rental customers (such as customer names, contact numbers, and optional uploaded ID/profile photographs) that you upload for identification and security.
* **Device Security Information:** During startup, the app checks if the device is rooted or jailbroken to ensure runtime integrity and block usage on compromised devices.
* **Payment Logs:** While subscription payments are processed securely by our third-party payment partner (PayHere), we store basic transaction logs (payment ID, amount, and success status) on our servers to manage your subscription state. We do not store credit card or raw banking credentials.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including to:

* Provide, operate, and maintain the features and functionalities of the TakenR app.
* Manage your business subscription and process payments through our payment gateway provider.
* Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
* Detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues (such as runtime app tampering or fraud).
* Ensure compliance with our security and licensing terms.

---

## 3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or business data to third parties. We only share information with third-party service providers in the following limited circumstances:

* **Payment Processing:** We use PayHere for subscription billing. Their privacy policy governs the processing of payment details.
* **Cloud & Authentication Services:** We use Firebase (Google) for secure user authentication and database synchronization.
* **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

---

## 4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/TLS) to transmit data between the mobile application and our servers, and secure local storage mechanisms for authentication tokens. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Data Deletion and Retention

We retain your business data only for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with the application services. If you wish to delete your account or request the permanent deletion of your business and customer data, please contact us at the support email provided below.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
